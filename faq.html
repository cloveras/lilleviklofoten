Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

Where is Lillevik Lofoten?
On Gimsøy, centrally between Svolvær and Leknes — a quiet, scenic base for exploring the whole archipelago. See Location.

How far is it from the airports?
Approx. 35 minutes from Svolvær (SVJ) and Leknes (LKN); about 3.5 hours from Harstad/Narvik (EVE). See Travel.

Are roads open year‑round?
Yes. Both E10, bridges and all roads on Gimsøy are maintained all year. In winter (Oct–Apr), use proper snow tires. See Winter at Lillevik.

How many guests and bedrooms?
Up to 7 guests in 4 bedrooms — comfortable for families or small groups, and popular for couples too. See room details on the main page.

How many bathrooms?
One full bathroom (shower + WC) plus an additional WC. See room details on the main page.

What amenities are included?
Beachfront with private beach area · free, fast Wi‑Fi · fully equipped kitchen (dishwasher/oven/coffee maker) · washer & dryer · fireplace · terrace/balcony · garden · free on‑site parking · free EV charging. See full details on the booking platforms.

Is there a sauna or hot tub?
No — but there is a sauna at Hov beach, 1 km from the house.

Is it family‑friendly? Accessibility?
Yes, family‑friendly (children’s high chair and baby cot available). 

House rules (pets, smoking, parties)
No pets. Non‑smoking property. No parties/events. Please follow the house rules shown during booking.

When can I see the Northern Lights?
Typically late August–early April on clear, dark nights. See northern lights at Lillevik Lofoten.

When is the Midnight Sun?
Late May–mid‑July. See summer at Lillevik.

Kayaking and rentals
Direct sea access for guests bringing their own kayak. Nearest kayak rental: Henningsvær (about 35 minutes). See Activities.

Parking & EV charging
Free private parking on site for up to three cars; free EV charging available.

How do I book?
Book via Booking.com, Airbnb or Lofoten Vacation – see Booking.

Check‑in / Check‑out
Check-in after 17, check-out before 10. Details are shown on your booking platform and in your confirmation.

More information
See the Lillevik Lofoten house book (PDF, 150+ pages) for much, much more details about the house, the surrounding area and general Lofoten tips and recommendations.
